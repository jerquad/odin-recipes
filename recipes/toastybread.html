Toasty Bread

Description

What is a proper meal without piping hot fresh bread? Not a meal I want!

Ingredients

  • A loaf of your favorite bread, I recommend something long and crusty
  • Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Get the oven goin' real hot, maybe broil? Or close to it, whatever.
  2. Slice the bread, you can go perpendicular if you like order and maximizing your bread usage. I like that. Some people will slice it diagonally, this makes for "bigger bread" which is great, but if you are not using the whole loaf that's a lot more surface to get stale.
  3. Put the slices into the over. On the rack is fine, who cares?
  4. Do not forget about the bread, take 'em out when the bread is "good".
  5. (Optional) spread butter to one side of the bread. Avoid the temptation to Butter the other side as well.
