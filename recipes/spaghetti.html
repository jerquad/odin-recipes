Spaghetti

Description

Don't let anyone tell you that you need to make your own spaghetti. That's maybe a fun activity if you have nothing else to do, but the store bought dehydrated kind is honestly it's own thing and it is wicked tasty. Also not pretentious.

Ingredients

  • Box of your choice of dehydrated spaghetti
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fill a big pot with water and add salt. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add the dry spaghetti. Make sure it's all nice and underwater
  3. In a couple of minutes, once the spaghetti is soft, start regularly pulling out strands and eating them.
  4. Once the strands are done, or even better, almost done, drain the pasta into a collendar (sp? lol). Tada, you made spaghetti!
